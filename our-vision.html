About us

let us know about our reading and cultural activities

St.Vincent's Academy, Purba Bardhaman owned by krishnam foundation, founded in 2023, Chaired by Chairman Mr.Sanjay kumar Gupta.

  • Our Vision

    The institution strives to develop those qualities that will make them global citizens seeking for innovative changes for excellence and committed to the progress of every individual.

  • Our Mission

    Our education system sustains strong moral examples as the core values of the school including respect, honesty, compassion and global citizenship to unite the world as one family.

Invest in education invest in the future

Education is the foundation of a brighter and more progressive future. By investing in quality learning, we empower individuals with knowledge, skills, and values that shape not only their personal growth but also the development of society as a whole. A strong educational system nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and global awareness, preparing students to face real-world challenges with confidence. When we invest in education today, we are building leaders, innovators, and responsible citizens for tomorrow.
